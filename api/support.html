Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Open the app and tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your registered email address to receive a 6-digit reset code.

How do I view my boarding pass?

Go to the "My Trips" tab, select your upcoming flight, and tap "View Ticket" to see your digital boarding pass and QR code.

My payment failed, what should I do?

Please check your internet connection and ensure your payment method has sufficient funds. If the issue persists, contact support with your booking reference if available.

How do I join MC Club?

You are automatically enrolled in MC Club upon creating an account. You can view your points and tier status in the "Profile" tab.
